About Us

Our Story

A Legacy of Exellence

Founded in 2015 by Eng. Paul Karara Ngunjiri, Oville Associates was established with a singular mission: to provide innovative, cost-effective, and sustainable engineering solutions to the East African market.

Over the years, we have grown from a small consultancy to a powerhouse in Civil and Structural Engineering, trusted by major corporations and government bodies alike. Our approach is rooted in a deep understanding of local challenges combined with global best practices.
